Biography

Haotian Xie is currently pursuing his Master of Science in Engineering in Industrial Engineering and Logistics Management at The University of Hong Kong. He obtained his Double Bachelor's degree in Systems Science and Engineering and Finance (Fintech) from Beijing Normal University in 2024. His research primarily focuses on Data-driven and human-centered decision-making, Supply chain and transportation management, Nonequilibrium statistical physics and information theory.
