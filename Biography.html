Biography

Haotian Xie is currently pursuing her Ph.D. in Computer Science at The University of Hong Kong. She obtained her Master's degree in Artificial Intelligence from Tsinghua University in 2024 and earned her Bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering from Peking University in 2021. Her research primarily focuses on machine learning and graph neural networks.
