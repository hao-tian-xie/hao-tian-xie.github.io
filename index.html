About Me

I am currently a Research Assistant at the Department of Industrial and Systems Engineering (ISE), The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU).

I received my Master's degree from HKU in 2025 and my Bachelor's degree from BNU in 2024. My research focuses on the intersection of Operations Research and Complex Systems, specifically applying data-driven optimization and network theory to solve problems in logistics and supply chain finance.
