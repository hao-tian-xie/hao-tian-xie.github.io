About Me

I am currently a Research Assistant at the Department of Industrial and Systems Engineering, The Hong Kong Polytechnic University. I received my Master's degree from The University of Hong Kong in 2025 and my Bachelor's degree from Beijing Normal University in 2024.

My research interests lie at the intersection of Operations Research and Complex Systems. Specifically, I focus on Data-driven Decision Making, Logistics & Supply Chain Management, and Complex Networks Theory. I am passionate about applying mathematical modeling and optimization techniques to solve real-world problems in supply chain finance and transportation systems.
