About Me

I am currently a Research Assistant at the Department of Industrial and Systems Engineering, The Hong Kong Polytechnic University.

I received my Master of Science degree from The University of Hong Kong in 2025 and my Bachelor of Science degree from Beijing Normal University in 2024. My research focuses on the intersection of Operations Research and Complex Systems, specifically applying data-driven optimization and network theory to solve problems in logistics and supply chain finance.
